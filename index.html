📲 Install app

Add Campervan Control to your home screen to launch it full-screen and use it offline — no app store, no account.

On iOS, open in Safari, tap the Share button, then Add to Home Screen. (Bluetooth control itself needs the Bluefy browser.)

About

Two independent Bluetooth systems, controlled from one app. The fridge (Alpicool) and the power station (Fossibot) each connect separately — connect whichever you're near.

Needs Chrome/Edge (Android/desktop) or Bluefy (iOS), Bluetooth on, and a secure (https) page. Works great on a Pixel.
